I am pursuing my PhD degree in the College of Intelligence and Computing from Tianjin University, Tianjin City, China, under the supervision of Prof Tie Qiu(The recipient of the National Science Fund for Distinguished Young Scholars). I obtained my BS and MS degrees from Zaozhuang University, China, in 2019 and Shenyang Aerospace University, China, in 2024, respectively. Now, I have finished three work about UAV-aided terrestrial computing, Digital Twins task scheduling, these work have been published on IEEE Transactions on Computers, IEEE Transactions on Network Science and Engineering and 2023 ICCSN, respectively.

My current research interests include distributed algorithms, wireless networking, edge intelligence and Digital Twins.

I'm looking forward to collaborating with you all. If you are interested in my work, please contact me via my email.
